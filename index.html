🍽️ DESIGN THINKING - CANTEEN PRE-ORDER SYSTEM
1️⃣ EMPATHIZE
Students face long queues, limited break time, and order confusion in canteens.
⬇
2️⃣ DEFINE
Problem: Long waiting time + crowding during lunch breaks reduces productivity.
⬇
3️⃣ IDEATE
Solutions: Pre-order app, QR pickup, digital menu, canteen selection system.
⬇
4️⃣ PROTOTYPE
App includes login, menu with images, cart system, order tracking, and QR code.
⬇
5️⃣ TEST
Reduces waiting time, improves efficiency, and improves student satisfaction.
